Highspeedinternet Data managment

Full-Stack

One of our websites (Highspeedinternet.com) was having difficulty keeping their data up to date. Our data was updated based off updates from our telecom partners, and those updates would roll in at random intervals. Sometimes multiple times a week. Each of those updates, required a ticket into the developers and a script being run on the back end to take a CSV and update all the data. I automated that entire process, by building a secure admin dashboard that allowed non technical users to upload the CSV and run the update script with a single click. I also implemented a notification system that would alert the team if any errors were encountered during the update process, as well as proper transaction handling ensuring that data integrity was maintained at all times. Due to the increased speed of updates, our "data-accuracy" skyrocketted and we were able to sign millions of dollars worth of new business from smaller telecom partners.

Technical Modernization

Infrastructure & Security

Many of our front end sites were running on legacy Rackspace hosting with a Wordpress CMS. This was a huge security risk and also made it difficult to implement modern features and optimizations. I led the transition away from that legacy infrastructure and towards a modern, containerized infrastructure. I also implemented a custom CMS built on Laravel and Storyblok that allowed for more flexibility and control over our content. This transition not only improved our security posture but also allowed us to implement new features and optimizations that were not possible with our old infrastructure.
